About

My name is Jani Tuononen and I'm Bachelor of Business Administration in ICT

I became interested in data analytics because I realized how huge amounts of data about us are being collected everyday and I started to wonder if we could utilize all that information. I think data analytics can be used to make reliable predictions because it is mostly based on numbers, not opinions.

I’m currently learning data science. I have done data science projects on Kaggle and I have also completed two certificates: “Elements of AI” & “Building AI”. I’m increasing my data science experience in Jyväskylä University of Applied Scineces course “Data-analytics and artificial intelligence”.
